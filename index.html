Volca Sample Breakbeat Embalmer

Use the live demo below, or download the project from github for offline use.

Breakbeat Embalmer uses the procedure detailed by electronic music producer Chris Lody to prepare a breakbeat for use on the Korg Volca Sample:

  1. Measure the length of a breakbeat audio file in samples [WAV preferred].
  2. Calculate the correct amount of silence to add to the end of it, by multiplying its length by .11111.
  3. Add that amount of silence to the original file, and render a downloadable WAV.
